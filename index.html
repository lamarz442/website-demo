We Provide Medical Services That You Can Trust!

At DVLR’s Therapeutics, we are developing viral immunotherapies that are designed to cause in situ vaccination against the unique antigens and neoantigens presented when tumor cells lyse and stimulate activation of both innate and adaptive immune mechanisms. The patient and tumor-specific memory response has been shown to produce a systemic and sustained anti-cancer effect based on a highly individualized T cell response against the injected tumor and uninjected metastases, and has the potential to change disease outcomes across a variety of indications..

We Are Always Ready to Help You & Your Family

DVLR Therapeutics pioneers viral immunotherapies, activating the immune system within tumors. Our platforms, CAN-2409 and CAN-3110, target solid tumors and glioblastoma.
